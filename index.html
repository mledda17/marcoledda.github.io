Short Biography

Marco Ledda is a Ph.D. candidate in Systems and Control at the University of Cagliari, Italy. He earned his Bachelor’s degree in Electric, Electronic, and Computer Engineering from the University of Cagliari in 2022, followed by a Master’s degree in Computer Engineering, Cybersecurity, and Artificial Intelligence, graduating with honors in 2024 from the same institution. His research focuses on system identification using neural networks, learning-based techniques for control systems, adversarial learning in control, and model predictive control (MPC).
